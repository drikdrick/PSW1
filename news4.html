9 Activities of Development Implementation Year 2017

15 Desember 2017


Based on the Government Work Plan (RKP) Ciburial Village Year 2017 which is the fifth year of RPJMDesa (Village Mid-term Development Plan) 2013-2019 Ciburial Village. Field Implementation of Village Development in 2017 implementing 9 Types of Activities. Amount of Budget (Amount of Amendment Amendment) of Village Development Implementation Year 2017 is Rp.1.394.069.000, -.

9 Activities of Development Implementation Year 2017 Ciburial Village mentioned above is as follows:

  1. Village Road Construction Activities
    Village Road Development Activities in 2017 is allocated from three sources of funds, namely Village Fund of Rp.675.000.000, - ADD for Rp.79.588.000, - and Self Funding Rp.174.000.000, -. Total Budget allocated for Village Road Construction activities is Rp.928.588.000, -. Road segment that will be built, that is Ruasa Jalan Pakar Timur, Cibengang Road, West Pakar Road (RW 07) and Pasanggrahan-Barutunggul (RW 11).
  2. Office of Physical Infrastructure Development Activitiesr
    Source of Fund for Implementation of Facility Development Activities and Physical Infrastructure Office sourced from the allocation of Village Funds (ADD) amounting to Rp.45.000.000, -, namely the Procurement Cabinet Archive Village Office and Procurement Sound System Multipurpose Building Ciburial Village.
  3. Development Activities of Sanitation and Sanitation Facilities
    Sanitation Sanitation and Sanitation Facilities Development Activities are sourced from Bandung District Financial Assistance Fund (PSPM Raksa Desa) for Rp.42.000.000, - and Self Funds amounting to Rp.9.000.000, -. Development Activities of Sanitation and Sanitation Facilities consisting of SPAL Development Activities RW 01, 03, 08 and Activities of Rehabilitation of MCK RT 02 RW 06.
  4. Maintenance Activities of Village Apparatus Facilities
    Maintenance Activities of Village Apparatus Facilities in the form of Procurement of PC Computers and Repair / Maintenance Routine Facilities and Infrastructure of Village Apparatus. Source of Funds Maintenance Activities Facility Apparatus of the Village comes from the Profit Sharing Fund of Tax / Levy Area of Rp. 14.960.000, -
  5. Development Activities / Development of Clean Water Facility
    Development Activities / Development of Clean Water Facility derived from the Village Fund Rp.140.021.000, - and ADD Rp.13.500.000. The amount of ceiling for activity budget of Development / Development of Clean Water Facility is Rp.153.521.000, -
  6. Development / MCK Building Activities
    Activity Development / Development of MCK with budget amounting to Rp.11.000.000, -. Source of funds to carry out the activities of Development / Development of MCK consists of two sources, namely Financial Aid Kabupaten Bandung (PSPM Raksa Desa) Rp.8.000.000, - and Swadaya Rp.3.000.000, -.
  7. Development Activities / Development of Environmental Infrastructure Facility
    Activities of Development / Development of Environmental Infrastructure Facility with budget amount Rp.10.000.000, -. Source of funds to carry out activities of Development / Development of Environmental Infrastructure Facility in the form of Construction / Rehabilitation of Gang Road RT 05 RW 05, ie from Village Fund Allocation (ADD).
  8. Village Communication and e-Government Development Activities
    Communications Development and Village e-government activities with a budget amount of Rp.5.000.000, -. Source of funds to carry out Communications Development and Village e-Government Activities, namely from the Tax Revenue / Retribution Fund.
  9. Village Infrastructure Improvement Activity
    The Improvement of Village Infrastructure is a Provincial Assistance for the Improvement of Village Infrastructure, which is in the form of Development of Conference Room of Ciburial Village Office. Source of funds for the implementation of Village Infrastructure Improvement Activities of Rp.150.000.000, - and Swadaya of Rp.25.000.000, -.
