Village Ciburial As Pioneer Village Website at Festival ICT Bandung Regency - News Ciburial

August 24, 2017


Office of Information and Communication Communication (Diskominfo) Bandung regency held the Festival of Information and Communication Technology (Festival of ICT) 2017. Bandung Regency Bandung ICT Festival 2017 held for one day at the Moch.Toha Complex Bandung District Government Soreang, Tuesday (December 12, 2017) .

Bandung Regent Dadang M. Naser said Bandung ICT Festival 2017 is a new activity for the first time conducted in Bandung regency. According to Dadang as an effort to improve public services and development of Information and Communications Technology (ICT) in Bandung Regency, the government should implement the ICT in various sectors of public services.

Regent Bandung appreciate Diskominfo Bandung Regency that has successfully facilitate as many as 260 village website (village online). This will facilitate the process of public communication, information distribution and open space public service transactions by the government, as well as community activities. The remaining 10 villages that are not online must be immediately dionline as well.



This is an effort to increase acceleration in terms of utilization of information and communication technology. With respect to public services, all villages and districts should boost performance, only 10 villages will be driven online. Later public service in Bandung regency will be more excellent,

Bupati bright accompanied by Head Diskominfo Atih Witartih ..

The Regent further emphasized that the existence of the village website is also able to strengthen the program of Bandung Regency Thousand Villages. In addition to facilitate the performance of public services, people who want to get services will also be more effective if ICT has been applied.

In the future, the government can provide the best service for the community. Applications that will be applied in each department, sub-district and village, such as APBD, UN, health and other services, please Dadang.

Regent hopes that with the implementation of ICT in Bandung regency, the government and the community can work together to utilize it, including to boost the economy through the program of Bandung Regency Thousand Kampung that has been running some of its existence.



The Bupati conveyed that the utilization of information and communication technology (ICT) is one of the priority programs of Bandung Regency Government. Many benefits can be gained from the utilization, such as increasing government accountability in providing services and access to information for the community and increase community participation in the development of Bandung regency.

Head of Diskominfo Atih Witartih added that the one-day event presented representatives of SKPD including Sub-district of Bandung Regency, village representative of Bandung Regency, with various speakers such as from Telkom University and ICT Volunteer. Bandung ICT Festival Festival 2017 itself consists of various activities such as Seminars, Workshops, Art Performances, Village Website Competition, ICT Stand, and others.

With the village website, we encourage the optimization of ICT for public information disclosure, as well as the development of the potential of each village.

said Atih.


At the Bandung Regency Bandung ICT Festival 2017 also presented awards for four award winning villages in the Village Website 2017 Contest as follows:

  1. Ciburial Village Cimenyan District (ciburial.desa.id) as Village Website Pioneer ;
  2. Cigentur Village Paseh Sub District (cigentur.desa.id) as the First Winner;
  3. Cipinang Village, Cimaung Sub District (cipinang.desa.id) as the Second Winner;
  4. Cibodas Village Pasirjambu Sub District (cibodas.desa.id) as the 3rd Winner.
