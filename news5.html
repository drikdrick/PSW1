3 Years of Utilization of Village Funds in Ciburial (2015-2017) - News Ciburial

December 29, 2017


& emsp; Dana Dana is one of the real implementation of the nine priority agenda that President Joko Widodo and Vice President Jusuf Kalla (Nawacita) want to achieve. Village Funds as set forth in Law No. 6 of 2014 About this Village mandated, the central government must budget the Village Fund of ten percent from and outside the transfer to the region.

& emsp; In practice, the Village Fund budget can not be immediately fulfilled by ten percent, but it is done in stages. The Village Fund in APBN 2015 is budgeted at 1.42 percent, in APBNP 2015 budgeted at 3.23 percent, in APBN 2016 budgeted at 6.50 percent, and in APBN 2017 can only be fulfilled by 10 percent.

& emsp; In APBN 2017 which has been legalized, the value of funding of the allocated Village is Rp.60.000.000.000.000, - and President Joko Widodo plans the village fund for 2018 doubled to Rp.120.000.000.000. 000, -. This proves the government's seriousness to build Indonesia from the periphery.
