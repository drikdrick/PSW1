13 Community Development and Empowerment Activities - Ciburial Newst

December 18, 2017


Based on the Government Work Plan (RKP) Ciburial Village Year 2017 which is the fifth year of RPJMDesa (Village Mid-term Development Plan) 2013-2019 Ciburial Village. Field Development and Village Community Empowerment in 2017 implement 13 types of activities. 13 types of activities consist of 3 types of activities of Community Development and 10 types of Community Empowerment Activities Activities.

The Amount of Budget (Indicative Ceiling Amendment) for Community Development and Community Empowerment in 2017 is Rp.293.382.700, -. The amount of the budget consists of the budget for Community Development of Rp.69.500.000, -, and the Community Empowerment budget of Rp.293.382.700, -.

13 The Community Development and Empowerment Activities of 2017 Ciburial Village mentioned above are as follows:

  1. Field of Community Development:
    Community Development Activities in 2017 is budgeted at Rp.69.500.000, -, which includes the following three activities:
    • Youth and Sports Development Activities
      Youth and Sports Development Activities in 2017 is budgeted for Rp.31.000.000, -. The budget is sourced from three sources of funds, namely (1) Revenue Sharing Tax / Levy (PBH) of Rp.9.000.000, -; (2) Original Revenue (PAD) of Rp.12.000.000, -; and (3) Other Earnings (DLL) of Rp.10.000.000, -.
    • Women's Organization Development Activities / PKK
      Activity Guidance of Women's Organization / PKK in 2017 is allocated from Revenue Sharing of Tax / Levy (PBH) of Rp.18.500.000, -. Total number of Cultural and Cultural Development Budget Activities in 2017 amounting to Rp.18,500,000, -.
    • Cultural and Cultural Development Activities
      Cultural and Cultural Development Activities in 2017 is allocated from Revenue Sharing Tax / Levy (PBH) of Rp.20.000.000, -. Total number of Cultural and Cultural Development Budget Activities in 2017 amounting to Rp.20.000.000, -.
  2. Field of Community Empowerment: Indonesia Community Empowerment Activities in 2017 is budgeted at Rp.223.882.700, -, which consists of 10 types of activities as follows Community Empowerment Sector:
    Community Empowerment Activities in 2017 is budgeted at Rp.223.882.700, -, which consists of 10 activities as follows:
    • Village Head Training and Training Tools Village Head Training and Village Village Training Activities in 2017 is allocated from Tax Revenues / Levies (PBH) of Rp.4.688.700, -. Total number of Village Head Training and Village Facility Training Activities in 2017 amounted to Rp.4.688.700, -.
    • Community Institution Capacity Building Activities
      The Community Institution Capacity Building activity in 2017 is allocated from Tax Revenues / Levies (PBH) of Rp 10,000,000. Total number of Activities Capacity Building of Community Institutions in 2017 amounting to Rp.10.000.000, -.
    • Operational Activities of Village Community Empowerment Institute
      Operational Activities of Village Community Empowerment Institution (LPMD) in 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) of Rp.14.400.000, -. Total amount of Operational Activity Budget of Village Community Empowerment Institution (LPMD) in 2017 is Rp.14.400.000, -.
    • Village Race Event
      Village Race Activities in 2017 is allocated from Revenue Sharing Tax / Levy (PBH) of Rp.1.000.000, -. Total number of Village Rural Events Budget by 2017 is Rp.1.000.000, -.
    • Community Health Promotion Activities and Supporting Family Planning and Posyandu Activities
      Community Health Promotion Activities and Supporting Family Planning and Posyandu Activities in 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) of Rp.70,194,000. The total amount of budget of Community Health Enhancement and Supporting Activities of Family Planning and Posyandu service in 2017 is Rp.70.194.000, -.
    • Basic Education Improvement Activities Including Welfare Assistance for Non-Formal Teachers
      Basic Education Increasing Activities Including Welfare Assistance for Non Formal Teachers by 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) of Rp.22,800,000. Total Number of Articles of Increase in Basic Education Including Welfare Assistance for Non Formal Teachers by 2017 of Rp.22,800,000, -.
    • Scholarship Assistance Activities for Underprivileged School Children Scholarship Assistance for School Children who are Not Capable in 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) of Rp.16.000.000, -. Total amount of Scholarship Assistance for School Students Who Can not Budget in 2017 of Rp.16.000.000, -.
    • Event Activity Help Program Package Learning
      The Activity Aid for the Learning Package Program in 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) of Rp.15.000.000, -. Total amount of Activity Assistance Fund for the Implementation of Learning Packages Program in 2017 amounting to Rp.15.000.000, -.
    • Bhakti Gotong Royong Masyarakat's Month Activities Society Activity Month Bhakti Gotong Royong Society in 2017 is allocated from Revenue Sharing Tax / Levy (PBH) of Rp.5.000.000, -. Total number of Monthly Activity Budget of Bhakti Gotong Royong Masyarakat in 2017 is Rp.5.000.000, -.
    • BPD Allowance Activities
      The BPD Allowance Activities in 2017 is allocated from the Village Fund Allocation (ADD) amounting to Rp.64.800.000, -. Total amount of BPD Allowance Activities Budget in 2017 is Rp.64.800.000, -.
