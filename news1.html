Administrative Development Activities for RT and RW

December 24, 2017


Administrative Development Activities for RT and RW is held in an effort to improve the effectiveness of government service delivery, development and community development in Ciburial Village. Participants of Administrative Development Activities for RT and RW consisted of 53 RTs and 12 RW managers throughout Ciburial Village.



Administration Development Activities for RT and RW presents two resource persons, namely:

  1. Dra. Hj. Rohmiasih, M.Si (Head of Institutional and Community Participation DPMD Kabupaten Bandung)
  2. Asep Hermawan, S.H. (Head of District Government Section Cimenyan Regency Bandung)



In accordance with the provisions of Article 13 of Regional Regulation of Bandung Regency Number 12 Year 2007 regarding Community Institution, Rukun Tetangga (RT) and Rukun Warga (RW) is one kind of social institution.
