Small Industry Training Activities from DisPerIn

Small Industry Training Activities from DisPerIn


A total of 20 participants from Ciburial villagers participated in the Training of Las Workshop from the Department of Trade and Industry (Disperin) of Bandung Regency. Small Scale Engineering Development and Training Activities was held for 4 days from the date of 16 s.d. October 19, 2017 held at Ciburial Village Hall.

Small Scientific Development and Training Activities for the Ciburial Villagers are part of IKM Development Activities in Strengthening the Industrial Cluster Network in Small and Medium Industry Development Program organized by the Trade and Industry Office of Bandung Regency.
