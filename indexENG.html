Vision and mission

  • VISION :
    Vision of Ciburial Village is "COMMUNITY CITY OF SOCIAL AND PRIVATIVE CIBURIAL VILLAGE IN THE RELIGIUS AND ENVIRONMENTAL ENVIRONMENT AS EDUCATION AND TOURISM VILLAGE".
  • MISSION :
    For the realization of the vision is determined four efforts / ways or missions that will support the achievement of the vision are:
    1. MAKE A GOVERNMENT OF CLEAN, DECREE, AND TRANSPARENT VILLAGES AND ORIENTED TO THE OPTIMIZATION OF SERVICE TO THE COMMUNITY;
    2. IMPROVE SOCIAL WELFARE AND COMMUNITY ECONOMY;
    3. INCREASING THE QUALITY OF HUMAN RESOURCES BASED ON FAITH AND TAKWA;
    4. MAKING A CLEAN, SECURE, TERRIBLE AND REGULATED COMMUNITY ENVIRONMENT.
